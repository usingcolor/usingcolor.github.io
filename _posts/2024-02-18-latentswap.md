LatentSwap is a lightweight face swapping framework that generates latent codes for pre-trained generators without external datasets. It features a fast training process with a simple three-term loss, producing high-resolution results comparable to state-of-the-art models. The framework is compatible with StyleGAN2 and 3D-aware generators like StyleNeRF.
