SAO-Instruct is a model based on Stable Audio Open for flexible, free-form natural language audio editing. Trained on a novel dataset of audio editing triplets, it generalizes to real-world audio and unseen instructions. SAO-Instruct outperforms existing methods in subjective studies and achieves competitive objective performance. Code and weights are publicly available.
